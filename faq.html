Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours? ▼
We're open from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day.
Why choose us? ▼
At Patrick's Pizza, we pride ourselves on serving delicious, handcrafted pizzas made with the freshest ingredients. Our welcoming atmosphere, attentive staff, and dedication to quality make us the go-to spot for pizza lovers.
What type of products does your store offer? ▼
We specialize in delicious pizzas made with fresh ingredients, offering a wide variety of toppings, crusts, and special menu items to satisfy every craving.
What safety measures are in place at your store? ▼
Our store follows all health and safety protocols to ensure a safe and clean environment for our customers and staff.
Where are you located? ▼
We are located in Las Piñas, Pulang Lupa Uno, along Naga Road, inside the Wise Choice grocery store.
